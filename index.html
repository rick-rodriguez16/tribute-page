Jimmy Page

OBE

Musician - Song Writer - Record Producer

Here is a time line of Jimmy Page's life.

  • 1944 - Born in Heston, London
  • Early 1960s - Session musican in tracks such as "Diamonds", "I'm a Lover Not a Fighter", "I've Been Driving on Bald Mountain"
  • Late 1960s - Page joins the Yardbird as bassist, eventually switching to lead guitarist.
  • 1968 - Led Zeppelin is formed with Page, John Paul Jones, Robert Plant, and John Bonham.
